什么是 avnu？

AVNU 是一种去中心化交易协议，旨在提供最佳执行。
我们的使命是构建流动性基础设施，旨在为交易者和 dApp 提供最佳执行力。我们相信，为客户提供服务的唯一方法是为他们提供最具竞争力的价格，并获得最广泛的资产，同时提供树立行业标准的卓越用户体验。

What is avnu?


AVNU is a decentralized trading protocol designed to provide optimal execution.

Our mission is to build a liquidity infrastructure aimed at providing optimal execution for traders and dApps. We believe that the only way to provide services to customers is to provide them with the most competitive prices and access the widest range of assets, while also providing an excellent user experience that sets industry standards.

Part 1: swap coin



Open the app website  https://app.avnu.fi/en

Click on 'connect' in the upper right corner to connect to the wallet, select your wallet, mine is' argent x wallet ', I click on' argent x ', or if you are a helmet wallet, click on' braavos'.







选择你想交换的代币,目前有ETH USDC DAI WBTC USDT wstETH LORDS 代币可以交换,

你可以输入你想交易多少个以太坊，我有0.1个以太坊，我输入0.059以太坊交换成usdc，app自动计算可以换成121个usdc，下一步我们点击swap交换。右边显示的是流动性池子,AVNU会选择在starknet上最优惠的池子给你提供交易

Choose the token you want to exchange. Currently, ETH USDC DAI WBTC USDT wstETH LORDS tokens are available for exchange,


You can enter how many Ethereum you want to trade. I have 0.1 Ethereum, and I exchange 0.059 Ethereum for USDC. The app automatically calculates that 121 USDC can be exchanged. Next, we click on Swap to exchange. The liquidity pool displayed on the right will be selected by AVNU to provide you with the most favorable transaction on starknit







点击swap后，在钱包点击confirm，交易就完成了

After clicking 'swap' and 'confirm' in the wallet, the transaction is completed
