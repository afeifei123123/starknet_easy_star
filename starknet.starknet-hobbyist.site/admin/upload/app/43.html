什么是 orbiter

Orbiter Finance 是一个跨链桥，是 Layer2 的基础设施，提供低成本和几乎即时的传输。

Orbiter目前支持 Ethereum、StarkNet、zkSync、Loopring、Arbitrum、Arbitrum Nova、Optimism、Polygon、BNB Chain、ZKSpace、Immutable X、dYdX、Metis 和 Boba 之间的跨链传输。

你可以用是 orbiter将StarkNet多资产跨连到支持 Ethereum,Arbitrum..等其他链上,或者从其他Ethereum..等链上把资产跨到StarkNet上.（你也可以理解为是资产转移到其他链上）

What is an orbiter

Orbiter Finance is a cross chain bridge that serves as the infrastructure for Layer2, providing low-cost and almost instant transmission.

Orbiter currently supports cross chain transmission between Ethereum, StarkNet, zkSync, Looping, Arbitrum, Arbitrum Nova, Optimism, Polygon, BNB Chain, ZKSpace, Immovable X, dYdX, Metis, and Boba.

You can use Orbiter to cross connect StarkNet multiple assets to support Ethereum, ArbitrumWait for other chains, or from other EthereumCross assets onto StarkNet on the chain(You can also understand it as the transfer of assets to other chains)


我会分成两部分想你介绍orbiter的功能
第一部分: 从Arbitrum把ETH跨连到StarkNet
第二部分：StarkNet把ETH跨连到 Ethereum

I will divide it into two parts to introduce you to the functions of Orbiter
Part 1: Cross connect ETH from Arbitrum to StarkNet
Part 2: StarkNet Crossconnects ETH to Ethereum



第一部分: 从Arbitrum把ETH跨连到StarkNet


第一步，先点击链接你的小狐狸钱包。上面框是从哪里跨连。下面是选择你想跨连到的目标链。

Part 1: Cross connect ETH from Arbitrum to StarkNet

The first step is to click on the link to your Little Fox Wallet. Where does the upper box cross connect from. Below is the target chain you want to cross connect to.





我们现在尝试从Arbitrum把ETH跨连到StarkNet，所以我们第一个框选择，Arbitrum。第二个选择StarkNet。选择StarkNet的时候，会弹出你选用什么钱包，这里我们选择argengt x，如果你用的是Braavos钱包，你就选Braavos。

We are now trying to cross connect ETH from Arbitrum to StarkNet, so we will select the first box, Arbitrum. The second option is StarkNet. When choosing StarkNet, it will pop up which wallet you choose. Here, we choose argengt x. If you are using a Braavos wallet, you will choose Braavos.




然后输入你想跨连的ETH数量，我这里有0.1个ETH ，我点击MAX ，app会自动帮你输入减掉gas费用的0.0976，然后我们点击send发送。

另外，如果我们鼠标点击。可以查看你这次需要的gas费用。我们的跨连0.1从ARB到Starknet费用是2.34美金。

Then input the number of ETHs you want to cross connect. I have 0.1 ETHs here, and I click MAX. The app will automatically help you input 0.0976 minus gas fees, and then we click send to send.

Also, if we click on '?' with the mouse?. You can check the gas fees you need this time. The cost of our cross connect 0.1 from ARB to Starknet is $2.34.








我们点击send后出现这个页面，我们继续点击confirm and send

After clicking on send, this page will appear, and we will continue to click on confirm and send






然后在小狐狸钱包点击确定，然后等待一分钟左右，我们的0.1ETH就成功从arb跨链转移到Starknet了

Then click OK in Little Fox Wallet, and wait for about a minute for our 0.1ETH to successfully transfer from arb cross chain to Starknet.






第二部分：StarkNet把ETH跨连到 Ethereum


如果你想把StarkNet把ETH跨连其他链，比如到Ethereum。点击选择startnet和Ethereum。如果你想跨到其他比如arb，linea。你可以把选择Ethereum的操作替换成Arbitrum或者linea。然后输入你想跨连的数量，再点击send。


Part 2: StarkNet Crossconnects ETH to Ethereum


If you want to connect StarkNet to ETH across other chains, such as Ethereum. Click to select startnet and Ethereum. If you want to cross to other areas such as arb and linea. You can replace the operation of selecting Ethereum with either Arbitrum or linea. Then enter the number of connections you want to cross, and then click send. 




然后再次点击CONFIRM AND SEND

Then click on CONFIRM AND SEND again




然后在弹出来的argent x钱包界面点击cofirm，就完成了我们的资产从starknet跨连转移到Ethereum

Then click on coconfirm on the pop-up target x wallet interface to complete the cross connect transfer of our assets from starknit to Ethereum
