JediSwap 由 Mesh 社区领导。
Mesh 社区是一群因对透明和平等可访问性核心原则的共同信念而团结在一起的人。Mesh 社区的核心使命之一是在没有任何权威和审查的情况下，让每个人都能平等地获得金融机会。简单地说，Mesh 社区希望将 OpenFinance/DeFi 带给世界上的每个人。

What is JediSwap?


JediSwap is the first fully unlicensed composable AMM on Starknet (ZK Rollup on Ethereum), which is built on Starknet and allows users to exchange tokens and pool liquidity on Starknet


JediSwap is led by the Mesh community.

The Mesh community is a group of people united by a shared belief in the core principles of transparency and equal accessibility. One of the core missions of the Mesh community is to provide equal access to financial opportunities for everyone without any authority or scrutiny. Simply put, the Mesh community hopes to bring OpenFinance/DeFi to everyone in the world.


In addition, JediSwap has the largest lock volume of AMM in starknit. The built-in swap of the Argent X wallet is based on JediSwap, Powered by JediSwap. If your transaction amount is large, choosing JediSwap may result in lower slip points, reducing your slip point losses.

The first step in the box is to enter how many Ethereum you want to trade. I have 0.1 Ethereum, and I enter 0.06 Ethereum to exchange for USDC. Part 2, Adding Liquidity and Removing Liquidity


Adding liquidity can increase your returns, but fluctuations in currency prices may lead to free losses. In a market with small fluctuations in currency prices, it is recommended to add liquidity to earn a certain amount of income. In a bull market, I recommend choosing ETH-WBTC for liquidity because you will not miss the increase in BTC and ETH

Extract money from the liquidity pool


Click on pool, and we will see that we just added 80USDC and ETH. Then, click on manage, and two buttons will appear: add liquidity and remove liquidity. Add liquidity is to continue adding liquidity, and we can click on remove liquidity to retrieve the 80USDC and ETH we just added.
