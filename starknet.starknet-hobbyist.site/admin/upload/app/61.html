Ekubo is an AMM established on the starknet network, which can provide liquidity to earn profits. Additionally

Uniswap will invest 3 million uni (approximately $12 million) in exchange for 20% of Ekubo's future governance tokens

Part 1: Earnings from deposits



Open the app websitehttps://app.ekubo.org/

Click on 'connect' in the upper right corner to connect to the wallet, select your wallet, mine is' argent x wallet ', I click on' argent x ', or if you are a helmet wallet, click on' braavos'.




然后点击Manage Liquidity

Then click on Manage Liquidity



然后点击Create new position

Then click on Create new position




在这里你可以选择两种代币，作为流动性，我选择ETH和WBTC做流动性，然后点击NEXT

Here you can choose two types of tokens for liquidity, ETH and WBTC for liquidity, and then click NEXT



按照默认设置，再次点击next

Click next again according to the default settings



你可以点击这里，自动输入你的WBTC，dapp会自动输入你等价值的ETH，然后点击add liquidity

You can click here to automatically input your WBTC, dapp will automatically input your equivalent value ETH, and then click add liquidity



在钱包点击confirm，添加流动性赚取收益就完成了

Click 'confirm' in the wallet to add liquidity and earn income, and you are done
