What is an Element Market?


Element is an NFT trading market established on the starknet networkThe most gas efficient NFT trading venue


I will divide it into two parts to introduce the functions of Element to you

Part 1: Purchase NFT

Part 2: Selling NFT






第一部分:购买nft
打开app网站 https://element.market/starknet


右上角点击connect连接钱包，选择你的钱包,我的是argent x钱包，我点击argengt x，如果你是头盔钱包，点击braavos。

Part 1: Purchase NFT

Open the app websitehttps://element.market/starknet



Click on 'connect' in the upper right corner to connect to the wallet, select your wallet, mine is' argent x wallet ', I click on' argent x ', or if you are a helmet wallet, click on' braavos'.






然后切换成starknet网络

Then switch to the starknit network




然后点击Explore，然后点击Marketplace，左边是NTF集合列表，你可以任意点击进去查看NFT合集。

Then click Explore, and then click Marketplace. On the left is the list of NTF collections, where you can click anywhere to view NFT collections.



如果我们想购买starknet.id相关的NFT 我们点击这里

If we want to purchase starknet.id     We click here for NFT related to starknet.id




然后点击buy now

Then click 'buy now'




在钱包点击confirm，就购买NFT成功了

Click 'confirm' in the wallet to successfully purchase NFT





第二部分 出售NFT，点击你的头像，在点击My NFTs

Sell NFTs, click on your avatar, and then click on My NFTs



选择你想出售的NTF，点击List for sale

Select the NTF you want to sell and click on List for sale



然后输入你想出售的价格，我写0.01ETH，然后点击Listing

Then enter the price you want to sell, I write 0.01ETH, and click on Listing



钱包点击confirm和SIGN，你的NFT就在出售中了

Click on confirm and sign in your wallet, and your NFT is for sale
