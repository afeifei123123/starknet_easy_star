What is zkLend?


ZkLend is a native L2 money market protocol built on Starknet, combining zk roll up scalability, excellent transaction speed, and cost savings with Ethereum's security. You can deposit in ETH or USDC to earn income. You can also use the pledged ETH to lend USDC to your wallet.



I will divide it into four parts to introduce the functions of zkLend to you

Part 1: Depositing ETH to earn income and proposing ETH

Part 2: Using ETH Pledge to Lend USDC Tokens







第一部分: 存ETH赚取收益和提出ETH
打开app https://app.zklend.com/markets
点击connect

Part 1: Depositing ETH to earn income and proposing ETH

Open the apphttps://app.zklend.com/markets

Click on connect





选择你的钱包，我的是argent x钱包。

Choose your wallet, mine is the Argent x wallet.




然后点击supply，存入ETH赚取收益

Then click on 'supply' and deposit in ETH to earn income




然后输入你想存入多少以太坊。点击ENABLE & SUPPLY ETH

Then enter how much Ethereum you want to deposit. Click on ENABLE&SUPPORT ETH




然后在钱包界面点击confirm，就完成了存入以太坊赚取收益的操作

Then click confirm on the wallet interface to complete the operation of depositing into Ethereum to earn profits




现在尝试取出刚才都存入的以太坊，我们再次点击spuuly

Now try to retrieve the Ethereum that was previously stored, and we will click on spuuly again




然后选择WITHDRAW,然后点击max取出全部ETH，然后点击WITHDRAW ETH。

Then select WithDRAW, click max to retrieve all ETHs, and then click WithDRAW ETH.




在弹出钱包界面点击confirm，等30秒左右，你的ETH就取出来到钱包里面了

Click confirm on the pop-up wallet interface, wait for about 30 seconds, and your ETH will be taken out and placed in the wallet




第二部分： 使用ETH质押，借出usdc代币

需要刚才存ETH，才能借出usdc，如果你刚才已经全部取出ETH，你可以重新尝试先存入ETH。然后点击你想要借的什么币，我这个选择usdc，如果你想借出其他币，你可以在其他币右边点击BORROW

Part 2: Using ETH Pledge to Lend USDC Tokens


You need to deposit ETH just now in order to lend USDC. If you have already withdrawn all ETH just now, you can try to deposit ETH first again. Then click on the currency you want to borrow, and I will choose USDC. If you want to borrow other currencies, you can click BORROW on the right side of the other currencies




然后输入你想借多少USDC，你能借多少usdc是根据你存入多少ETH计算的，。最后点击BORROW USDC

Then enter how much USDC you want to borrow, and the amount of USDC you can borrow is calculated based on how much ETH you have deposited,. Finally, click BORROW USDC




然后在钱包点击confirm，等30秒左右，你借到的usdc就会出现在钱包上了

Then click on confirm in the wallet, wait for about 30 seconds, and the USDC you borrowed will appear on the wallet





然后归还借到的usdc，我们再次点击usdc旁边的borrow

Then return the borrowed USDC, and we will click on 'borrow' next to USDC again




选择repay，然后点击max。最后点击REPAY Usdc

Select 'replay' and then click 'max'. Finally, click on REPAY Usdc




然后钱包界面点击confirm。就归还usdc成功了。

Then click on confirm on the wallet interface. Successfully returned USDC.
