Part 1: Deposits

Open the app websitehttps://trade.brine.fi/

Log in to your metamask wallet first


然后点击desposit存款

Then click on deposit to deposit


选择stark usdc,然后输入usdc的数量,最后点击按钮

Select start USDC, then enter the number of USDCs, and finally click the button


然后链接starknet的钱包,并且在钱包点击confirm

Then link to starknit's wallet and click on confirm in the wallet
