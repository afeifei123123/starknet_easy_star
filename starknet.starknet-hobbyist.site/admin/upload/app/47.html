What is Nostra?


Nostra is the most commonly used lending liquidity agreement. Deposit your cryptocurrency into Nostra to earn income, and use your deposit as collateral to borrow cryptocurrency, while leveraging the unique features of the protocol.. You can deposit in ETH or USDC to earn income. You can also use the pledged ETH to lend USDC to your wallet.

Part 1: Depositing ETH to earn income and proposing ETH

Part 2: Using ETH Pledge to Lend USDC Tokens







第一部分: 存ETH赚取收益和提出ETH
打开app https://app.nostra.finance/
点击connect

Part 1: Depositing ETH to earn income and proposing ETH

Open the app https://app.nostra.finance/


Click on connect


选择你的钱包，我的是argent x钱包。

Choose your wallet, mine is the Argent x wallet.



然后点击这里，存入ETH赚取收益

Then click here to deposit ETH to earn income



然后输入你想存入多少以太坊。点击deposit

Then enter how much Ethereum you want to deposit. Click on deposit




然后在钱包界面点击confirm，就完成了存入以太坊赚取收益的操作

Then click confirm on the wallet interface to complete the operation of depositing into Ethereum to earn profits



现在尝试取出刚才都存入的以太坊，我们再次点击这里

Now try to retrieve the Ethereum that was previously stored, and we will click here again




然后选择WITHDRAW,然后点击max取出全部ETH，然后点击下面的WITHDRAW 。

Then select WithDRAW, click max to retrieve all ETHs, and then click WithDRAW below.




在弹出钱包界面点击confirm，等30秒左右，你的ETH就取出来到钱包里面了

Click confirm on the pop-up wallet interface, wait for about 30 seconds, and your ETH will be taken out and placed in the wallet






第二部分： 使用ETH质押，借出usdc代币

需要刚才存ETH，才能借出usdc，如果你刚才已经全部取出ETH，你可以重新尝试先存入ETH。然后点击你想要借的什么币，我这个选择usdc，如果你想借出其他币，你可以在其他币右边点击BORROW

Part 2: Using ETH Pledge to Lend USDC Tokens


You need to deposit ETH just now in order to lend USDC. If you have already withdrawn all ETH just now, you can try to deposit ETH first again. Then click on the currency you want to borrow, and I will choose USDC. If you want to borrow other currencies, you can click BORROW on the right side of the other currencies



然后输入你想借多少USDC，你能借多少usdc是根据你存入多少ETH计算的，。最后点击BORROW USDC，他这里要求最少借入500usdc，我钱包没那么多钱无法借入。如果你存入很多ETH就可以借入500udsc以上，然后点击下面的borrow就可以借入了

Then enter how much USDC you want to borrow, and the amount of USDC you can borrow is calculated based on how much ETH you have deposited,. Finally, click BORROW USDC. He requires a minimum of 500USDC to be borrowed here, and I don't have that much money in my wallet to borrow.
