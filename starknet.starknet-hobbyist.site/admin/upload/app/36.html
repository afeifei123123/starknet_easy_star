点击pool按钮

Part 2, Adding Liquidity and Removing Liquidity


Adding liquidity can increase your returns, but fluctuations in currency prices may lead to free losses. In markets with low currency fluctuations, it is recommended to add liquidity to earn a certain amount of income. In bull and bear markets, I still recommend holding BTC or ETH in spot


Click on the pool button






出现了很多流动性池,大家最喜欢添加的流动性是EHH-USDC，里面一共有200W美金池子我们再点击new position,

There have been many liquidity pools, and the one that everyone likes to add the most is EHH-USDC, which has a total of 200W US dollars in pools. Let's click on the new position again,




在这里我们选择刚刚购买的USDC和ETH ,USDC我输入刚才都90usdc，然后点击deposit，

你可以选择购买WBTC，然后用WBTC代替我刚才的90usdc去添加流动性

Here we select the USDC and ETH that we just purchased. I input both USDC and ETH as 90USDC, and then click deposit,

you can choose to purchase WBTC and use WBTC instead of my previous 90USDC to add liquidity







我们再次点击 confirm supply

We will click on confirm supply again




弹出来的钱包界面，点击confirm，等10秒左右 我们的钱eth和usdc就添加到10Kswap流动性池子去赚取收益了

The wallet interface pops up, click confirm, and after about 10 seconds, our money ETH and USDC will be added to the 10Kswap liquidity pool to earn profits

Extract money from the liquidity pool
