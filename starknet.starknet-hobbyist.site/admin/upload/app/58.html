什么是 Owlto Finance?

Owlto Finance是starknet的跨链桥，
提供Ethereum跨链到starknet网络.
或者starknet网络资产跨链回Ethereum.



我会分成两部分想你介绍Owlto Finance的功能
第一部分: 从Ethereum把ETH跨连到StarkNet
第二部分：从StarkNet把ETH提现到 Ethereum



What is Owlto Finance?


Owlto Finance is the cross chain bridge of starknit,

Provide Ethereum cross chain to starknit network

Or starknit network assets cross chain back to Ethereum




I will divide it into two parts to introduce you to the functions of Owlto Finance

Part 1: Cross connect ETH from Ethereum to StarkNet

Part 2: Withdrawing ETH from StarkNet to Ethereum





第一部分: 从Ethereum把ETH跨连到StarkNet.
先点击connect wallet,链接你的metamask钱包

Part 1: Cross connect ETH from Ethereum to StarkNet

First click on connect wallet to link to your metamask wallet


然后点击这里链接starknet的argent x钱包

Then click here to link starknit's target x wallet


这里输入你想跨连多少数量的ETH，然后点击send

Enter the number of ETHs you want to cross connect here, and then click send


然后点击confirm and send

Then click on confirm and send

在metamask点击确认，就跨连成功了

Click on confirm on metamask, and the cross connection is successful


第二  怎么从StarkNet把ETH提现到 Ethereum？

和刚才一样，首先

链接starknet的argent x钱包

链接metamask钱包

Form选择starknet，在To选择Ethereum

然后输入ETH的数量，点击send

How can I withdraw ETH from StarkNet to Ethereum?

Just like before, first of all

Target x wallet linked to starknit

Link Metamask Wallet

Select starknit for Form and Ethereum for To

Then enter the number of ETHs and click send


继续点击confirm and send

Continue clicking on confirm and send


在钱包页面点击confirm ，就完成了

Click 'confirm' on the wallet page to complete the task
