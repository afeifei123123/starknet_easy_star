什么是 layerswap

layerswap 是一个跨链桥，提供低成本和几乎即时的传输。



你可以用是 layerswap 将StarkNet多资产跨连到支持 Ethereum,Arbitrum..等其他链上,或者从其他Ethereum..等链上把资产跨到StarkNet上.（你也可以理解为是资产转移到其他链上）

What is layerswap

Layerswap is a cross chain bridge that provides low-cost and almost instant transmission.

You can use layerswap to cross connect StarkNet multiple assets to support Ethereum, ArbitrumWait for other chains, or from other EthereumWait for the assets on the chain to be transferred to StarkNet(You can also understand it as the transfer of assets to other chains)

Let me give an example, for example, connecting ETH from Arbitrum to StarkNet

The first step is to click on "from" and "to" to select the target chain. Cross connect ETH from Arbitrum to StarkNet

So choose Arbitrum from

To select StarkNet






然后选择选择Arbitrum和StarkNet

Then select Arbitrum and StarkNet





最后像这样子,然后输入你想要跨链的金额

Finally, like this, enter the amount you want to cross chain





在这里点击选择你starknet的地址

Click here to select your starknet address






然后选择你的starknet钱包

Then choose your starknet wallet





你的starknet钱包就自动显示在这里了,然后点击你的地址

Your starknet wallet will automatically display here, then click on your address





然后我们点击swap now

Then we click on swap now





继续点击connect ,链接你的metamask钱包

Continue to click connect and link to your metamask wallet






链接metamask钱包成功后,点击send from wallet

After successfully linking the metamask wallet, click send from wallet





最后在metamask钱包点击确认,你的arb的ETH就成功跨链到starknet了

Finally, click confirm in the metamask wallet, and your arb's ETH will successfully cross chain to starknet
