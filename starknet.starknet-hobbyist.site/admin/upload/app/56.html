Part 1: Purchase NFT

Part 2: Selling NFT





第一部分:购买nft
打开app网站 https://pyramid.market/

Part 1: Purchase NFT

Open the app websitehttps://pyramid.market/

Click on 'connect' in the upper right corner to connect to the wallet, select your wallet, mine is' argent x wallet ', I click on' argent x ', or if you are a helmet wallet, click on' braavos'.




然后切换成选择钱包

Then switch to selecting a wallet



然后点击COLLECTIONS,下面是然NTF集合列表，你可以任意点击进去查看NFT合集。也可以通过搜索选择你喜欢的NFT

Then click on COLLectiONS, and the following is a list of NTF collections. You can click on it to view the NFT collection at will. You can also search to choose your favorite NFT



选择一个NFT合集，点击

Select an NFT collection and click



然后选择一个你喜欢的NFT

Then choose a NFT you like



然后点击buy now

Then choose a NFT you like



然后在选择buy，在弹出钱包点击confirm，购买操作就成功了

Then select buy, click confirm when the wallet pops up, and the purchase operation is successful




第二部分 出售NFT，点击你的头像，然后在点击PROFILE，你就会看到你拥有的NFT，选择一张你想出售的NFT并且点击

To sell NFTs, click on your avatar, and then click on PROFILE. You will see the NFTs you have. Select an NFT you want to sell and click on it



然后点击SELL NOW

Then click on SEL NOW



先设置你想出售的价格，我设置0.01ETH，然后点击ENABLE COLLECTION

First set the price you want to sell, I will set it to 0.01ETH, and then click on ENABLE COLLECT



在钱包点击confirm

Click on confirm in the wallet



等1分钟再次点击，LIST NFT

Wait for 1 minute to click again, LIST NFT

 


钱包点击SIGN，你的NFT就在出售中了

Click Sign on your wallet and your NFT is for sale
