什么是 sithswap？

sithswap是基于 Starknet 构建的 AMM。您可以将 ETH 与 USDC 和 DAI 进行交换，并将您的加密货币存入流动性池中。

我会分成两部分想你介绍sithswap的功能
第一部分: 交换代币功能
第二部分：添加流动性跟移除流动性



What is sithswap?


Sithsnap is an AMM built on Starknet. You can exchange ETH with USDC and DAI, and deposit your cryptocurrency in the liquidity pool.


I will divide it into two parts to introduce the functions of sithswap to you

Part 1: Exchange token function

Part 2: Adding Liquidity and Removing Liquidity






第一部分: 交换代币功能

右上角点击connect连接钱包，选择你的钱包,我的是argent x钱包，我点击argengt x，如果你是头盔钱包，点击braavos。

Part 1: Exchange token function


Click on 'connect' in the upper right corner to connect to the wallet, select your wallet, mine is' argent x wallet ', I click on' argent x ', or if you are a helmet wallet, click on' braavos'. 







点击这里选择你想交换的代币,第一是你想用什么代币作为交换.第二是你最后想交换成什么币,支持ETH WBTC USDC USDT DAI wstEth互相交换。我点击ETH，第二步选择USDC，然后输入你想交换的数量,点击swap

Click here to select the token you want to exchange, the first is what token you want to use as an exchangeThe second is what currency do you want to exchange in the end, supporting ETH WBTC USDC USDT DAI wstEth mutual exchange. I click ETH, select USDC in the second step, enter the quantity you want to exchange, and click swap



然后在弹出的钱包界面点击confirm。我们的交易就成功了！

Then click confirm on the pop-up wallet interface. Our transaction was successful!






第二部分,添加流动性跟移除流动性

添加流动性可以为你增加收益,但是币价的浮动可能会导致无偿损失,在币价波动小的行情下,推荐添加流动性赚取一定的收益，

点击LIQUDITY按钮，然后点击add。我选择ETH 和USDC，你也可以用其他币添加流动性。然后输入你想添加到数量，最后点击第四步four的ADD


Part 2, Adding Liquidity and Removing Liquidity


Adding liquidity can increase your profits, but fluctuations in currency prices may lead to free losses. In a market with low fluctuations in currency prices, it is recommended to add liquidity to earn a certain amount of income,


Click on the LIMITED button, then click on add. I choose ETH and USDC, and you can also add liquidity using other currencies. Then enter the quantity you want to add, and finally click on the ADD of step four




这里点击confirm，就完成添加流动性了

Click 'confirm' here to complete adding liquidity






第三部分 提取流动性池子的钱

点击LIQUDITY,然后点击add。再次点你My Pools

我们会看到刚才添加到usdc跟eth,我们点击ETH/USDC。

Extract money from the liquidity pool


Click on LIMITED, then click on add. Click on your My Pools again

We will see that we just added ETH and ETH to USDC, and we will click ETH/USDC. 

然后点击WITHDRAW

Then click on 'WithDRAW'

然后点击MAX ，然后点击下面的WITHDRAW

Then click MAX, and then click WithDRAW below
