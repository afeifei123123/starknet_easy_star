What is FLEX?


FLEX is an NFT trading market established on the starknet network


I will divide it into three parts to introduce the functions of FLEX to you

Part 1: mint nft

Part 2: Purchase NFT

Part 3: Selling NFT





第一部分: mint nft
打开app网站 https://flexing.gg/


右上角点击connect连接钱包，选择你的钱包,我的是argent x钱包，我点击argengt x，如果你是头盔钱包，点击braavos。

Part 1: mint nft

Open the app websitehttps://flexing.gg/



Click on 'connect' in the upper right corner to connect to the wallet, select your wallet, mine is' argent x wallet ', I click on' argent x ', or if you are a helmet wallet, click on' braavos'.









点击mint，然后点击chose file选择电脑上的图片上传，

Click 'mint', then click 'choose file' to upload the image on the computer,



在电脑上选择你喜欢的照片

Choose your favorite photos on the computer




然后输入你图片的名字，我输入starknet123，然后点击Mint，然后在弹出钱包页面点击confirm，就完成mint操作了

Then input the name of your image, I input starknet123, click Mint, and then click confirm on the pop-up wallet page to complete the Mint operation
