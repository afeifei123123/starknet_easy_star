什么是 fibrous？

Fibrous，这是一个基于 Starknet 构建的 AMM 聚合器！我们的应用程序可以让您轻松找到所有 Starknet AMM 的最佳掉期利率。通过 Fibrous，您可以获得最具竞争力的价格。我们的用户友好界面旨在使 Starknet 上的交易更加高效，并为每个人提供便利

What is fiber?


Fibrous, this is an AMM aggregator built on Starknet!Our application allows you to easily find the best swap rate for all Starknet AMMs. Through Fibrous, you can get the most competitive prices. Our user-friendly interface aims to make transactions on Starknet more efficient and provide convenience for everyone

我们尝试用ETH和USDC进行交换

你可以输入你想交易多少个以太坊，我输入0.1以太坊交换成usdc，app自动计算可以换成209个usdc，下一步我们点击swap交换。会选择在starknet上最优惠的池子给你提供交易，

We are trying to exchange ETH and USDC

You can enter how many Ethereum you want to trade, and I will exchange 0.1 Ethereum for USDC. The app will automatically calculate that it can be exchanged for 209 USDC. Next, we will click on the swap exchange. We will choose the most favorable pool on Starknet to offer you transactions, 





点击swap后，继续点击confirm swap

After clicking swap, continue to click confirm swap




然后在钱包界面点击confirm，就交换成功了

Then click confirm on the wallet interface, and the exchange is successful
