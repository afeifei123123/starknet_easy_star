什么是 briq？

briq 是一个基于被称为 briqs 的基本元素的 NFT 工艺和可组合性系统。briqs 可以被组合以创建更复杂的结构，称为集合。
你可以铸造 briqs，使用这些 briqs 建造集合，将集合转让给别人，拆开集合来取回 birqs 并制造新的作品。
任何用 briqs 制作的东西都是 NFTs。你可以把 briq 看作是 NFT 的积木。组装你的 briqs 来创造结构，给 briqs 上色，在区块链上组装集合，然后把它转让给朋友。
如果你不喜欢你的建造，可以把它拆开，把 birq 拿回来，建造新的东西。
你的 briqs 和用它们建造的一切都属于你。你可以自由地做你想做的事。

What is briq?


Briq is an NFT process and composability system based on basic elements called briqs. Briqs can be combined to create more complex structures called collections.

You can cast briqs, use these briqs to build collections, transfer collections to others, disassemble collections to retrieve birqs, and create new works.

Anything made with briqs is NFTs. You can think of briq as a building block for NFT. Assemble your Briqs to create structure, color Briqs, assemble collections on blockchain, and then transfer them to friends.

If you don't like your construction, you can take it apart, take birq back, and build something new.

Your briqs and everything built with them belong to you. You are free to do what you want.
