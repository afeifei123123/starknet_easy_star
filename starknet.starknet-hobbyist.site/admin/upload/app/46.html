What is StarknetID?


StarknetID is a domain name protocol app on starknit that provides identity and naming services for starknit. You can use your preferred name instead of your wallet address to receive transfers from others. You can also trade and sell Starkid's ". Stark Domain Name" and "Mint's NFT" on the NFT trading market.

第一部分: 注册.stark域名
右上角点击connect连接钱包，选择你的钱包,我的是argent x钱包，我点击argengt x，如果你是头盔钱包，点击braavos。

Part 1: RegistrationStark Domain Name

Click on 'connect' in the upper right corner to connect to the wallet, select your wallet, mine is' argent x wallet ', I click on' argent x ', or if you are a helmet wallet, click on' braavos'.

 




然后在第一步方框内输入你想注册的域名，注册域名不能跟别人重复，如果starkid显示可以注册，点击第二步的available

Then enter the domain name you want to register in the first step box. The registered domain name cannot be duplicated with others. If the starkid displays "can be registered", click "available" in the second step






然后输入你的Email，你可以选择注册几年，每一年费用是0.009ETH。（注册三个或者四个字母数字的价格会更高，四个字母每一年0.085 ETH，三个字母的每一年价格是0.15 ETH）。我们选择点击REGISTER MY DOMAIN

Then enter your email and you can choose how many years to register, with a fee of 0.009ETH per year. The price for registering three or four letters and numbers will be higher, with 0.085 ETH per year for four letters and 0.15 ETH per year for three letters. We choose to click on REGISTER MY DOMAIN







在钱包点击confirm，就完成注册starkid了，以后可以根据你的xxxxxx.Stark替换你的地址发给你朋友作为收款方式了.

Click 'confirm' in the wallet to complete the registration of starkid. In the future, you can use your xxxxxxStark has replaced your address and sent it to your friend as a payment method

Part 2: mint StarknetNFT for id

StarknetThe ID has an NFT identity that allows for free mints. mints will have various different images, and these NFT image IDs are unique. They can also be linked to your Twitter and DC.

We clicked on MY Identities, and then clicked on ADD Identities






在钱包点击确认confirm，图片就mint完成了。一次的gas手续费大概0.1-0.2美金。跟一次转账的手续费差不多。

Click on 'confirm' in the wallet, and the image will be completed. The one-time gas handling fee is approximately $0.1- $0.2.
