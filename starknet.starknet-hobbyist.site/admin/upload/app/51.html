什么是 starkgate?

starkgate 是starknet的官方跨链桥，
提供Ethereum跨链到starknet网络.
或者starknet网络资产跨链回Ethereum.
starkgate目前支持的资产有ETH ,WBTC ,USDE,USDC,DAI,wstETH..等.



我会分成两部分想你介绍starkgate的功能
第一部分: 从Ethereum把ETH跨连到StarkNet
第二部分：从StarkNet把ETH提现到 Ethereum

What is starkgate?


Starkgate is the official cross chain bridge of starknet,

Provide Ethereum cross chain to starknit network

Or starknit network assets cross chain back to Ethereum

The assets currently supported by starkgate include ETH, WBTC, USDE, USDC, DAI, wstETHEtc




I will divide it into two parts to introduce the functions of starkgate to you

Part 1: Cross connect ETH from Ethereum to StarkNet

Part 2: Withdrawing ETH from StarkNet to Ethereum





第一部分: 从Ethereum把ETH跨连到StarkNet.
点击connect wallets.

Part 1: Cross connect ETH from Ethereum to StarkNet

Click on connect walls





然后点击get started

Then click on get started




选择你的钱包,点击metamask钱包

Select your wallet and click on Metamask Wallet




然后点击,connect starknet wallet

Then click on 'connect starter wallet'





选择你使用starknet的钱包,我安装的是argengt x,如果你是Braavos钱包，你就选择Braavos

Choose your wallet using starknit. I installed Argengt x. If you are a Braavos wallet, choose Braavos




然后注意，核对下的你argengt x和metamask钱包的地址是否一样，如果一样就点击continue

Then pay attention and check if the addresses of your argengt x and metamask wallets are the same. If they are the same, click continue




接下来输入你想要跨连到starknet的币

Next, enter the currency you want to cross connect to starknet

How can I withdraw ETH from StarkNet to Ethereum?

Just like before, first of all

Target x wallet linked to starknit

Link Metamask Wallet

Click on 'with aw' and then enter the ETH you want to propose from the starknet. Then click on Transfer. 





Argent x钱包弹出界面点击confirm。就完成了从StarkNet把ETH提现到 Ethereum的操作

Argent x wallet pop-up interface, click confirm. Completed the operation of withdrawing ETH from StarkNet to Ethereum
